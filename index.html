Alliot Nagle

Hello! I am in my last year as an undergrad at UW-Madison; in May 2019, I will graduate with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering.

I cherish the diverse experiences and opportunities I have been a part of as an undergrad at UW-Madison. Some of these experiences include my time as a music major before switching to EE, volunteering in Memphis, TN and Cincinnati, OH on two separate spring break trips, and working with the Clean Lakes Alliance to develop a predictive model for algal blooms on Madison's lakes.

My research interests lie broadly within machine learning, but I am most interested in constructing efficient algorithms for solving the optimization problems used by machine learning models. Furthermore, I am interested in select fields which may intersect with machine learning: information theory, signal processing, control theory, and distributed systems.

I have many interests and I hold an appreciation for the arts, mostly for music. Some of my interests and hobbies include weight training, nutrition, reading, running, and playing drum set. I enjoy listening to and learning about music of the African diaspora, namely the music of Cuba and Brazil.
