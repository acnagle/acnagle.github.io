Alliot Nagle

Hello! I am a second-year PhD student in the ECE department at UT Austin, and I am advised by Prof. Hyeji Kim. During my years as an undergraduate and master's student in the ECE department at UW-Madison, I was advised by Prof. Dimitris Papailiopoulos.

Currently, my research interests lie within A.I. foundation models and their domain-specific applications, and methods for efficient training and inference.

I have many interests and I hold an appreciation for culture-rich music. My interests and hobbies include weight training, nutrition, reading, running, biking, and playing drum set. I enjoy listening to and learning about music of the African diaspora, namely the music of Cuba and Brazil.
